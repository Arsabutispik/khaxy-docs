Log settings are used to configure the logging channels in your server.

## Message Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Message Delete, Message Edit, and Message Bulk Delete.

## Guild Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Member Join, Member Leave,
Member Kick, Member Ban, Member Unban, Guild Update (e.g. Name changes etc.),

## Member Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Timeout, Nickname Change, Role Update (e.g. Role Add, Role Remove, Role Create, Role Delete),

## Channel Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Channel Create, Channel Delete, Channel Update

## Voice Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Voice Channel Join, Voice Channel Leave, and
Voice Channel Move.

## Emoji Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Emoji Create, Emoji Delete, and Emoji Update.

## Role Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Role Create, Role Delete, and Role Update.

## Sticker Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Sticker Create, Sticker Delete, and Sticker Update.

## Event Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Event Create, Event Delete, and Event Update.

## Invite Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Invite Create and Invite Delete.

## Poll Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Poll Create, Poll End, Poll Delete, and Poll Vote.

## Stage Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Stage Create, Stage Delete, and Stage Update.

## Soundboard Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Sound Create, Sound Delete, and Sound Update.

## Thread Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Thread Create, Thread Delete, and Thread Update.

## Webhook Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Webhook Create, Webhook Delete, and Webhook Update.
