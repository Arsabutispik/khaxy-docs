Log settings are used to configure the logging channels in your server.

## Message Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Message Delete, Message Edit, and Message Bulk Delete.

## Member Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Member Join, Member Leave,
Member Kick, Member Ban, Member Unban, and Member Update (e.g. Nickname changes etc.).

## Voice Logs
The channel where the bot will log the following events: Voice Channel Join, Voice Channel Leave, and
Voice Channel Move.
